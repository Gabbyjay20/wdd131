LearnHub Academy · 90-Day Learning Roadmap
=========================================

1. Vision snapshot
   - Describe the role or project you are targeting in one bold sentence.
   - List three capabilities that define success in that role.

2. Weekly rituals
   - Focus Sprint (2x per week)
   - Feedback Loop (1x per week)
   - Reflection Log (every weekend)

3. Progress signals
   - Skill evidence: links to code, decks, or prototypes.
   - Confidence rating: score each week from 1-5.
   - Community check-in: tag an accountability partner.

4. Obstacles + safeguard plan
   - Time blockers → Assign a boundary or automation.
   - Knowledge gaps → Flag them for mentor office hours.
   - Energy dips → Pair concepts with movement or music cues.

5. Celebrate
   - Every 30 days share a "Before vs After" snapshot.
   - Write one paragraph of gratitude to future you.
