Site Name: LearnHub Academy

Why this name? The name "LearnHub Academy" was chosen because it clearly communicates the site's purpose as a central hub for learning and education. The word "Hub" suggests a gathering place where multiple resources and opportunities converge, while "Academy" conveys professionalism, quality education, and a structured learning environment. Together, they create a memorable brand that appeals to students, educators, and lifelong learners seeking a trusted platform for online education.

Optional Domain: learnhub-academy.com or learnhubacademy.org

Site Purpose

LearnHub Academy serves as a comprehensive online learning platform that connects students with high-quality educational resources and courses. The site provides:

  • Course Catalog: A searchable database of online courses across various subjects including technology, business, arts, and sciences
  • Student Resources: Study guides, downloadable materials, and interactive learning tools
  • Enrollment System: Easy-to-use registration and enrollment forms for prospective students
  • Instructor Profiles: Information about qualified educators and their areas of expertise
  • Community Features: Discussion forums and networking opportunities for learners
  • Progress Tracking: Tools for students to monitor their learning journey and achievements

The primary goal is to make quality education accessible and engaging for adult learners seeking professional development, career advancement, or personal enrichment.

Scenarios

These scenarios represent questions from our target audience that will help drive the content and structure of the website:

Scenario 1: What courses are available for someone looking to transition into web development?

A working professional in their 30s wants to change careers and is interested in learning web development. They need to understand what courses are offered, the prerequisites, course duration, and whether the curriculum is suitable for beginners with no coding background.

Scenario 2: How do I enroll in a course and what is the cost structure?

A prospective student wants to understand the enrollment process, payment options, course fees, and whether there are any discounts or financial aid available. They need clear, step-by-step guidance on how to register and start learning.

Scenario 3: Can I access course materials on mobile devices while commuting?

A busy professional with a 45-minute daily commute wants to know if they can study effectively on their smartphone or tablet. They need information about mobile compatibility, offline access, and the user experience on smaller screens.

Font Hierarchy

  • H1: 2.5rem - 3rem (Page titles)
  • H2: 1.8rem - 2rem (Section headers)
  • H3: 1.3rem - 1.5rem (Subsection headers)
  • Body: 1rem (16px base) (Main content)
  • Small: 0.875rem - 0.9rem (Captions, footnotes)

Wireframe Design Principles

  • Mobile-First Approach: Content stacks vertically for easy scrolling on smaller screens
  • Responsive Navigation: Hamburger menu on mobile expands to full navigation bar on desktop
  • Hero Section: Prominent welcome message with clear call-to-action above the fold
  • Content Hierarchy: Featured courses displayed prominently with testimonials for social proof
  • Sidebar Elements: Enrollment form and news sidebar on desktop, integrated into main flow on mobile
  • Footer: Comprehensive links and information accessible on all devices
